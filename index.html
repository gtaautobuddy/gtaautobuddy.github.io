About Me

I am a home-grown mechanic that has worked on my own vehicles as well as friends & neighbours. My skills mainly specialize in imports (Toyotas, Hondas, Nissans and Subarus) and am familiar with all types of engine designs - Inline 4, Flat 4, Inline 6, V6, V8 configurations and more. Anything from oil changes to engine rebuilds and everything in between, I have done it all. I've been stuck in situations when purchasing vehicles second hand and want an informed second opinion, but have no one to rely on. This is where my services come in to fill the void. With GTA Mobile Auto Buddy, my services will give you the reassurance you need to purchase or walk away from a prospective vehicle purchase.

Services

My service offerings are simple. You will email me the vehicle ad ahead of time and I will give you my opinion on the vehicle, a FREE initial assessment and recommendation. Should you decide to continue with going to inspect the vehicle and want my second opinion, I will charge a fee for my services. The in person vehicle inspection will include:

1. Exterior Vehicle report
2. Interior Vehicle report
3. 21-point vehicle inspection (fluid levels, brake pad inspection,
tire pressure, under vehicle inspection)

A report will be provided to you as well as a informed-opinion on what to expect for repairs down the road for the vehicle. I will also notify to you if there will be any challenges required to certify and emissions test the vehicle of your choice.

Philosophy

My philosophy is to empower you with the confidence you need to buy this vehicle, or at least know what you are getting yourself into. I am a believer of value for your money and this is what separates me from other mobile mechanics. I don't charge an arm and a leg for my services. I do this because I want other people to make informed decisions at an affordable cost.
